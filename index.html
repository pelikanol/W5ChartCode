Visualizing 30 Years of Meat Consumption in China

This project visualizes the meat produced for human consumption in the China between 1991 and 2024. Data sourced from OECD for different types of meat show that pork is the perenial favorite with a bit of a dip over COVID. Next and slowly rising in popularity, particularly over COVID, is chicken, followed by beef and sheep meat.

Data Source: MakeoverMonday 2020/W21
